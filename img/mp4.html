Welcome 2 Karachi

Welcome 2 Karachi is an Action, Comedy movie starring Hayley-Marie Axe, Nigel Barber, Arshad Warsi and Adnan Shah Tipu. Welcome 2 Karachi is directed by Ashish R. Mohan, released on 28, May, 2015. On a drunk night, Shammi suggests that he and Kedar leave for America on a cruise, even though they laugh it off in that instance, in a freak accident by the sea, Kedar and Shammi who are supposed to manage a wedding baraat on a cruise, leave without the baraatis only to lose their direction and land up in Karachi, Pakistan.
